Spanish Translation Prioritization Rubric

Evaluate a story's potential for translation to effectively serve Spanish-speaking & Latino communities.

How to Use This Form

This rubric is designed to help you objectively evaluate a news story's suitability for translation. By scoring each article, you can ensure we are prioritizing content that is most relevant, timely, and impactful for our Spanish-speaking audience.

  1. Read each question carefully, as well as the section's overall definition.
  2. Use the horizontal slider for each question to select a score from 1 to 5. The descriptive words below the slider correspond to scores 1, 3, and 5 to guide your selection.
  3. After scoring all questions, click the "Evaluate Priority" button.
  4. The form will automatically calculate the total score and determine the article's translation priority.

Relevance & Impact (Total Possible: 20)

"Relevance & Impact" evaluates whether a story is essential for the daily lives and civic participation of the Latino community. A story with high relevance provides crucial information that helps community members make informed decisions, access resources, or understand events that directly affect them.
